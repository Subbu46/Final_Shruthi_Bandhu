About Us

Our Story

Shruthi Bandhu was founded by a team of passionate engineers and accessibility advocates who recognized the challenges faced by mute and hard-of-hearing individuals in daily communication. Inspired by real-life stories, we developed an AI-powered glasses system that translates gestures into speech, empowering users to express themselves freely.

Our Mission

To bridge the communication gap for the deaf and mute community through innovative technology, making the world more inclusive and accessible for everyone.
